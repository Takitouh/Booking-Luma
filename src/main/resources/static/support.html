Support page.

Common questions.

  1. How to book a room?

    To book a room you need to go to the home page and select the hotel you want, then you will see a button that says "Book now", click on it and follow the instructions.

  2. How to cancel a booking?

    To cancel a booking you need to go to the home page and select the hotel you booked, then you will see a button that says "Cancel booking", click on it and follow the instructions.

  3. How to contact support?

    You can contact support by sending an email to support@luma.com
